Sensitive-data protection

Cortex scans free text before it's stored, shared, or sent to any model, and masks regulated identifiers. It records that redaction happened — never the raw value. Only the identifier is replaced; surrounding context is preserved.

PHI is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256-GCM, per-tenant keys), and Cortex operates as a HIPAA Business Associate under a signed BAA. Customer data is never used to train models. Read the full security, HIPAA & BAA details →
